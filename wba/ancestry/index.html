What's Bad About Ancestry

Don't let Ancestry.com test your DNA. It will sell the information. You, or your relatives, could be denied medical insurance as a result.

Ancestry advertised MyFamily.com as a secure place to store family memories, then shut it down and discarded all the text people had posted.

You can use network services to communicate information, but don't depend on them as a place to preserve it.
