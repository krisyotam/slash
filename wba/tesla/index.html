What's Bad About Tesla

Reasons not to buy from Tesla

The worldwide boycott is very strong in Europe and is making a significant dent in the US too. Join in!

  • Tesla is owned by Elon Musk, a.k.a. "the muskrat", an overt Nazi sympathizer who works with other enemies of democracy in the U.S. He is conspiring with other billionaires to cut off medical coverage for tens of millions of Americans. He supports far-right-wing extremists in other countries too.

  • Tesla sells an add-on software feature that drivers are not allowed to use.

    This practice depends on a back door, which is unjust in itself. Asking users to buy something years in advance to avoid having to pay an even higher price later is manipulative.

  • Tesla tracks whoever drives their cars.

  • On the mysterious fatal accidents of Tesla cars, which the company releases little data about.
